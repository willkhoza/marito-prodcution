zokusebenzela. Isibonelo:
• Uma kudingeka abafundi bakokelezele impendulo efanele, bacebise ukuthi babeke uphawu
kuqala empendulweni efanele. Kumele babuze uthisha ukuthi yimpendulo efanele yini
leyo ngaphambi kokuthi bayibhale ezincwadini zabo.

badedele basebenzise izincwadi zokubhalela (exercise books) ezinemigqa baze bakwazi ukubhala
